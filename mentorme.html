MentorMe

UX/UI, Web, Concept

IDEA & TASK

This app invites the user to choose content that can improve his or her mood. In addition, the user creates a personal profile and his own collection of books, music, etc. And he can also track changes in his emotions over the course of a week or month.

UI DETAILS

The colors give a sense of freshness and modernity to the design, aimed at the 20+ audience. Contrasting fonts in style were used for the headlines. And the 3D illustrations are part of the overall style of the site.

LOGIC

Black, white and pink as accent colors were chosen as the main colors. An accent font was used in the headings. Illustrations and icons are made in Cinema 4D.
